**__Rules__**

1. Treat everyone with respect. Absolutely no harassment, witch hunting, sexism, racism, or hate speech will be tolerated.

2. No spam or self-promotion (server invites, advertisements, etc) without permission from a staff member. This includes DMing fellow members.

3. No age-restricted or obscene content. This includes text, images, or links featuring nudity, sex, hard violence, or other graphically disturbing content.

4. No offensive or inappropriate nicknames, profile pictures, or bios. Moderators and staff reserve the right to change nicknames or pfp.

5. If you see something against the rules or something that makes you feel unsafe, let staff know. We want this server to be a welcoming space!

6. No help vampiring or overt negativity. Complaining about the game is allowed in moderation.

7. No chat or mention spam. Self-promotion is allowed in moderation.

8. All rule enforcement is at the discretion of the mod team and staff. If you are unhappy with how the server is ran, you are free to not participate.
